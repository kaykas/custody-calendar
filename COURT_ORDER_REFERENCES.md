### Holiday/School Break Schedule

#### Halloween
- **Page**: 4
- **Section**: 16a
- **Text**: "Halloween: with Mother in odd years and Father in even."

#### Thanksgiving Break
- **Page**: 4
- **Section**: 16b
- **Paragraph**: Lines 22-27
- **Text**: "Thanksgiving break: To be shared equally, with mid-break exchange at Noon on Wednesday (the day before Thanksgiving).

  - ii. In even years, Mother shall have the first half of the break and Father shall have the second."

- **Page**: 5
- **Section**: 16d

  - "iii. In odd years, Father shall have the first half of the break and Mother shall have the second."

## Implementation Notes

### Holidays NOT in Court Order
The following holidays are NOT specified in the court order Section 16 and have been removed from the implementation:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day (July 4th)
- Labor Day

**Court Order Section 16 ONLY specifies**: Halloween, Thanksgiving Break, Winter Break, and Spring Break.

## Priority System

Based on the court order's implicit hierarchy:
1. **Priority 200**: Winter Break 2025 specific schedule (Section 16c)
2. **Priority 150**: Special events (Parents' birthdays, Mother's/Father's Day) (Sections 17-18)
3. **Priority 100**: Holiday breaks (Halloween, Thanksgiving, Winter 2026+, Spring) (Section 16)
4. **Priority 50**: Summer vacation schedule (Section 14)
5. **Priority 20**: Weekend schedule (Section 12b)
6. **Priority 10**: Regular weeknight schedule (Sections 12a, 13a)
